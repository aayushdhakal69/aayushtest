About Bsc.CSIT and Syllabus

BSc.CSIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology) is a popular undergraduate program in Nepal that focuses on the study of computer science and information technology. The program typically takes four years to complete and covers a wide range of topics, including programming, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, database management, software engineering, web development and much more.
