Why HCC

  1. Human Resource

    2. HCC is a top employer in the education, technical, management, and support fields. Many of our graduates plan to work in the education sector, specifically at HCC.
    Our Human Resource Management Unit, which was created to manage and develop teaching and non-teaching human resources, is always on the lookout for new leaders and talents to join HCC.

  2. Reasonable and competitive prices

    3. Our service fees are significantly lower than those charged by the majority of Nepalese education firms. This is due to the fact that we are an independent education firm that sets our own rates; as a result, our rates are fair and competitive without sacrificing quality in any way.

  3. Standing

    4. Since its inception, HCC has earned a stellar reputation for providing first-rate services and collaborating with prestigious universities and colleges all over the world. As a result, pursuing an education at HCC will increase your chances of landing a better job in the future.

  4. Partnership and Collaboration

    5. Today, HCC has formed alliances with leading organizations in the fields of information technology as well as innovation, business, entrepreneurship, research, and education. Every year, several programs and events are organized in collaboration with HCC or elsewhere for our students. These collaborations have resulted in mutual benefits for HCC and partner organizations in the areas of business development, innovation, and learning.

  5. Research and Development

    6. HCC relies heavily on research and development. We have been working on several initiatives, collaborating on various research projects with various leading partner organizations.
